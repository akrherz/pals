Developing Science Literacy

The development of effective learning environments in science, like the MT206 meteorology course, requires a willingness to change instructional paradigms and view the role of technology in light of these new paradigms. The goal of this project is to engage the student in the process of science rather than memorize the product of science. The computer is used to enable the student to do scientific investigations that were not previously feasible.
