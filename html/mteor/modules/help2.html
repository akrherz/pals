The Meanings of Curves

    The left white line represents the vertical profile of dew point temperature. The white line on the right is temperature. The yellow line represents a parcel that is lifted from the surface. The lower portion follows a dry adiabat (a straight line on this diagram) but when the parcel reaches saturation, it then follows a curved saturation adiabatic line.

    When the temperature of air parcel is warmer than enrironmental air, it is less dense than the surrounding air and is bouyant.
