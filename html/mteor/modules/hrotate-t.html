Explanation of Rotation
 
    The animation below shows a huge paddle wheel spinning in the atmosphere which helps us see the effects of wind speed change with height.  The length of the arrows represents wind speed.   This change of wind speed with height is called wind shear. 

    The presence of this wind shear results in  horizontal rotation of the wind (as shown by the paddle wheel) and becomes an important factor in the development of the severe thunderstorm.
