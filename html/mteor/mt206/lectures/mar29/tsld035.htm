    El Niño is an exaggeration of the usual seasonal cycle. During the El Niño in 1986-1987, you can see the warm water (red) penetrating
    eastward in the Spring of 1987. There is another El Niño in 1991-1992, and you can see the warm water penetrating towards the
    east in the northern hemisphere spring of 1992. The El Niño in 1997-1998 is a very strong El Niño.
