Apparently cooling the air involved in clouds is important

  • One cooling mechanism we learned about last period was adiabatic cooling. It is associated with rising air

  • When air rises it cools at 10 C/1000m when no drops (condensation) happens

  • It cools at ~ 6 C/1000m when drops are formed (condensation occurs and we see a cloud)
