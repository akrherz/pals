Calm Winds

    When most of the ground’s radiation is lost, its temperature
    can drop rapidly. The air just above the ground also cools
    dramatically. If there is no wind, the air above the ground can
    continue to cool; however, if it’s windy the cool air near the
    ground mixes with warmer air higher above the ground, which
    negates the cooling by the ground.
