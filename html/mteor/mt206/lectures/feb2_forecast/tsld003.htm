How can I forecast for Wind Direction and Speed at 18 Z

  • Analyze the surface chart:

    • Winds around low pressure are counterclockwise (cyclonic flow)
    • Winds around high pressure are clockwise (anticyclonic flow)
    • Winds ahead of a cold front have a southerly component (SE, S, SW)
    • Winds behind a cold front have a northerly component (W, NW, N)
