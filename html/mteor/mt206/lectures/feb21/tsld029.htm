PPT Slide

    On a recent sunny day with snow on the ground, at 3pm the
    temperature was 28F and the vapor pressure was 5mb. The
    saturation vapor pressure at 28F is 7mb. When you get up
    the next day there is widespread fog.
    What do you think happened?
    Group responses:
    The vapor pressure increased (19 groups)
    The temperature decreased (25 groups)
    Melting or evaporating snow increased the vapor pressure (14 groups)
