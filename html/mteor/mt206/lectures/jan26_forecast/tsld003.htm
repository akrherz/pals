Forecasting - Reviewing Concepts

  • Persistence Forecasting- What happens today will happen tomorrow. This is a good method if there are no highs, lows, or fronts affecting the forecast area.

  • Observational Forecasting- Observing the weather upstream (N, NW, W, SW, S) of the forecast area and estimating its movement during the 24 hour forecast period.
