Small Group Question

    One recent sunny day with snow on the ground
    at 3pm the temperature was 28F and the vapor
    pressure was 5mb. The saturation vapor pressure at 28F is 7mb. When you get up the next day there was wide-spread fog.
    What do you think happened? Play detective
    and identify the important clues and discuss
    the implications of them. (Hint: Draw graph that includes the saturation curve and plot the values given.)
