Severe Text File Help Page

This product helps you to see all the severe weather statements that have been issued so far by the various National Weather Service Offices across the country for that day. Here, you will find information about watches, warnings,
hazerdous weather outlooks, as well as storm damage reports.

HINT To quickly find watches and warnings look for the following within text file:

WATCHES

BULLETIN - IMMEDIATE BROADCAST REQUESTED
(Type of watch and its id number)
STORM PREDICTION CENTER NORMAN OK
(time & date)

WARNINGS

BULLETIN - IMMEDIATE BROADCAST REQUESTED
(Type of warning issued)
NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE (location)
(time & date)
