What is Z, UTC, and GMT times?

The prime meridian (or zero degrees longitude or the Universal Coordinate), passes through Greenwich, England. Military terminology for zero is z, or the word zulu. Thus there are several common ways people refer to this longitude for use as a time reference.

    Zulu (Z)

    Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)

    Universal Time Coordinate (UTC)

The following table may be helpful to convert from one of these times to local time. For Des Moines, IA, in the winter (CST), 18Z is 12 PM (noon).
