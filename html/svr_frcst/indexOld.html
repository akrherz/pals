The Severe Weather Forecasting Exercise is a web-based exercise that is designed to stimulate interest in severe weather. This exercise is being developed for use by all levels of meteorologists and K-12 students as well. Because of the wide range of skill among users, there are two versions of the forecasting exercise. Watch this page for updated cases and new features...
