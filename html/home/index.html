Dr Carlson's Cool Clouds for Kids:

 [ https://pals.agron.iastate.edu/carlson ]

If you have not checked this out, you really should!! His Cool Clouds for Kids page contains many of his photos that he has taken throughout the years. Check it out...

Campbell Automated Weather Stations:

[ https://pals.agron.iastate.edu/campbell ]

The ISU Campbell Stations homepage contains current and archived weather data from 10 stations accross Iowa.

ISU Meteorology Forecasting Contest:

[ https://pals.agron.iastate.edu/frcst ]

This is a brand new web-application that I designed in Sept of 99. The forecasting contest is used and managed on-line by Meteorology 311 and 411 students. The system manages their forecasts and grades them after validation is entered by an administrator.

ISU Meteorology Homepage:

[ http://www.meteor.iastate.edu ]

Currently, Steve Decker (sgdecker@iastate.edu) is working on the new ISU Meteorology Homepage. It is currently a work in process, but most things on the PALS server are just that...

National Forecasting Contest:

[ http://www.meteor.iastate.edu/nfc ]

This is another web-based daily weather forecasting activity. This web-app was developed in Oct 99 and still needs alot of cosmetic work. I hope to intergrate a local grading system as well.
