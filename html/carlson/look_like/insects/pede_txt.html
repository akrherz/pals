Walking Time


Some insects have many legs. They are called centipedes (100 legs) or millipedes (1000 legs). This photo shows a part of a long, long millipede passing by. Quick, count the legs before its gone! I count 9 crooked legs. What would it cost to buy fancy sneakers for this guy?
