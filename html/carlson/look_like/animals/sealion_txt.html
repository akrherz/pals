Something in the Ocean


Off the coast of California, my daughter Laura tells me there are a lot of sea lions swimming around playing in the cool ocean. This one is certainly blowing a lot of bubbles. Can you see its flippers? What sound does a sea lion make? Do they bark like a dog?

This photo was in the Aug/Sept 1997 issue of "Country" magazine.
