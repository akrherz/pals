A Tale about Horses


There is an old song that goes, "Bows and flows of angels hair, and ice cream castles in the air." This photo could easily be "angel´s hair". But, I see horses tails, like the horses I had when I was young: Bill, Boots, Rocky, and Bullet. Have you ridden on a horse lately? It is a blast!
