A Candy-dog Cloud


Here a poodle dog sits on its behind observing the sky and the many clouds that are floating by. I bet he is looking to play some dog games with "Snoopy." Others who look at this cloud have said that it reminds them of a very large "chocolate candy kiss" with a ribbon at the top. Which do you see, or prefer?
