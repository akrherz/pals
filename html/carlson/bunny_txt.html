The Energizer Rabbit

Of all my photos, this one is a favorite. I see the energizer rabbit taking a break. Where is the drum? I always thought that this rabbit just kept going, and going, and going, and going.....

This photo was in the Aug/Sept 1997 issue of "Country" magazine.
